Essential Islamic Studies Course

$0 / course

  • “Essential Islamic Studies Course” is a diploma Course that would help sisters to understand and get familiar with the most essential aspects of Islam, which would allow them to consolidate the basic understandings and practising upon Islam. The course will cover three crucial subjects providing an in-depth study of ‘Aqeedah, Fiqh, and Social Ethics. It is a part-time, 26-week course. The classes will be held online every Monday and Thursday from June 1st to August 31st, 6:00 pm to 6:40 pm (Edmonton, Canada time). The course will be conducted by qualified and certified ‘Alimah
