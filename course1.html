Essential Islamic Studies Diploma Course

  • This course would help sisters to understand and get familiar with the most essential aspects of Islam, which would allow them to consolidate the basic understandings and practising upon Islam. The course will cover three crucial subjects providing an in-depth study of ‘Aqeedah, Fiqh, and Social Ethics. It is a part-time, 12-weeks course. The classes are being held online, every Monday and Thursday, from June 1st to August 31st, 6:00 pm to 6:40 pm (Edmonton, Canada time). The course is being conducted by qualified and certified ‘Alimah along with the daughter of Sheikh al-Tafseer, Maulana Khalil-ur-Rahman Sajjad Nomani DB, spokesperson of All India Muslim Personal Law Board.
  • The course is guided and supervised by The Jurist of the Age, Maulana Khalid Saifullah Rahmani, founder member of All India Muslim Personal Law Board (AIMPLB) and general secretary of Islamic Fiqh Academy.
