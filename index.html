About Us

       
       
         
           

              The Fajr Islamic Center is an online islamic center for Islamic studies and spirituality based on modern-tradition methodology.

This center is run by qualified 'Alimah. Our goal at The Fajr Islamic Center is to enlighten our intellects and souls through the sacred knowledge of Qur'an and Sunnah.

Qualified 'Alimas teach classes for children, girls and sisters in Urdu and English via teleconference and internet (for select courses).

Our aim is a succession in our students' Islam, Iman, and Ihsan.
